Exploring logical policy angles with datasets and X insights. Not your typical resume—more like a think tank preview.

About

This is where I break down complex policy issues using data analysis, build automated tools for news aggregation, and explore the intersection of technology and governance.

Through various projects and experiments, I'm building systems that can help understand and navigate the information landscape more effectively.

Current Focus

  • Automated News Analysis: Using AI tools to process and summarize trending topics with real-time sentiment tracking.
  • Research Experiments: Building interactive dashboards for data analysis using SQL, Python, and automated pipelines.
  • Policy Modeling: Exploring sovereignty policies through data simulations and economic analysis.
  • Technical Writing: Documenting the process and insights through articles and analysis pieces.

Approach

Instead of traditional analysis, I focus on building systems that can:

  • Process large amounts of data automatically
  • Provide real-time insights into public discourse
  • Model policy impacts through simulation
  • Make complex topics more accessible through visualization
